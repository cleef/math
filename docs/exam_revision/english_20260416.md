## words

  
school bag / notebook / crayon / glue / brush

## grammar
Whose is this?
It’s mine / yours / his / hers

## sentences
Whose bag is this?
It’s Kitty’s.
It’s hers.

## conversation

A: Whose bag is this?
B: It’s Kitty’s.
C: It’s hers.

A: Whose notebook is this?
B: It’s mine.
C: It’s his.

## Tricky points
主语
He is Peter.

形容词性代词
This is his bag.
That is his book.

名词性代词
This bag is his.
That book is his.

宾格
I like her.
This is for her.

形容词性代词（她的 + 名词）
This is her bag.
That is her pen.

名词性代词
This bag is hers.
That pen is hers.


| 人     | 形容词性（+名词） | 名词性（单独用） |
| ----- | --------- | -------- |
| 我 I   | my bag    | mine     |
| 你 you | your book | yours    |
| 他 he  | his pen   | his      |
| 她 she | her bag   | hers     |
